Principles of Design

Visual Hierarchy

SALUDSA

The hierarchy does not come only from the size and can be taken from the commercial objective, they are those small messages distributed by specific parts of the page where we want to give a specific message to the user and invite him to do a certain action.

White Space and Clean Design

HUAWEI

The correct amount of space is an important element of web design. It allows objects to exist within it. The correct amount of white space makes a website look clean and modern and you can more easily identify the hierarchies and the main message that the site wants to convey.

PARC: Alignment

CREHANA

The elements on a page should be aligned with one another with reasonable reference in that the organization of items improves the readability and thus overall user experience on the page and site.
