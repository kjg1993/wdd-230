Chamber of commerce Guayaquil

Target Audience

Chamber of Commerce of Guayaquil is aimed at those large and small businessmen and entrepreneurs who are just starting their way, to promote competitiveness and open new markets both nationally and internationally, always having as a short, medium and long-term objective. and with the sole purpose of developing the maximum potential of our partners.

Personas

Magdalena Rodriguez

Magdalena is a 65-year-old lady who has a sewing position, she yearns to have more staff to help her since only she works in her small sewing workshop, Magdalena needs training and guidance on how to obtain more influx of customers in order to obtain more income and thus have the capacity to have more staff in their position.

Jimmy Ruiz

Jimmy Ruiz is a 45-year-old father of a family, owner of a gardening and pest control company, his company has already been in the market for several years in recent years his company has grown little by little but he needs contacts and information to reach companies larger and with greater purchasing power.

Scenarios

Hacer un préstamo a un banco

Mrs. Magdalena wants to train you how to make a presentation to acquire a loan to a Bank, so she remembers that one of the services that the Guayaquil Chamber of Commerce provides is training for entrepreneurs who want to do their business.

Expand to other areas of the city

Jimmy wants to expand to other parts of the city in the future, and he knows that with the support of the Guayaquil chamber of commerce he will have support to train and seek strategic places where he can locate.

Implement new technologies

Create a company

Jimmy, in his desire to innovate, has been thinking of creating a new small company that is responsible for buying fruits from the interior of the country to bring them to his city, and he has found that the chamber of commerce helps him with legal and legal advice to the creation of this new venture.
