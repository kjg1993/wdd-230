Target Audience

The target audience of the polarweather.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Students, people who work in public transport, merchants, farmers, people who work in the area of health and other dependencies, people who live in high-risk areas.
  • Age: 16 to 95.
  • Technology: help our visitors to make better decisions through the daily information of the most accurate forecasts possible, working with highly qualified people to solve problems and providing effective solutions in a timely manner.
  • Income: income ranging from $ 4,000 annually to $ 72,0000.
  • Motivation: Motivationpositively impact the lives of our visitors, through effective forecasts that seek to facilitate their lives.
