Our Purpuse

Chamber of Commerce of Guayaquil is aimed at those large and small businessmen and entrepreneurs who are just starting their way, to promote competitiveness and open new markets both nationally and internationally, always having as a short, medium and long-term objective. and with the sole purpose of developing the maximum potential of our partners.
